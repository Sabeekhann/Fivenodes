The challenge

What makes this hard

Every industry has its own engineering traps. Here are the ones that catch most teams building in this space.

01

Real-time tracking generates enormous data volumes

Thousands of GPS pings per minute require purpose-built ingestion infrastructure, not general-purpose databases.

02

Route optimisation is computationally hard

Naive implementations do not scale. The vehicle routing problem requires appropriate algorithmic approaches.

03

Third-party carrier APIs are unreliable

Integration with FedEx, UPS, DHL, and last-mile carriers involves undocumented edge cases and frequent API changes.

04

Operational visibility is fragmented

Data lives in a TMS, a WMS, carrier systems, and spreadsheets. Operators cannot see the whole picture.
